Inventory Overview:

Display current stock levels for all products with options to search and filter by product, category, or warehouse.
Show alerts for low stock and upcoming expiries.
Batch Management:

Track inventory by batch numbers and display expiry dates.
Provide alerts for batches nearing expiry and allow for quick actions like reallocating stock or marking it for clearance.
Warehouse Location Management:

Manage and update the specific locations of products within the warehouse (aisle, shelf, bin).
Offer optimized picking routes for order fulfillment based on product location within the warehouse.
